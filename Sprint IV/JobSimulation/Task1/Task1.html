About

Our College is a multidisciplinary research university with multiple campuses across India. Amrita is ranked A++ by NAAC and is the 7th Ranked University in India as per the National Institution Ranking Framework (NIRF) for 2024, it also one of India's leading research universities. The institution is headquartered in Ettimadai,Tamil Nadu, and has campuses in Four Indian states: Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham organises a full-fledged campus placement campaign with MNCs and reputed companies' world over every year. 92% of students secured positions with 200 companies, attaining a highest CTC of 56.95 LPA and an average CTC of 9 LPA.
